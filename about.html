Dora

Early design experience began in high school through participation in school-based projects creating items such as hats and cups, developing familiarity with the full process from concept to finished object. This foundation continued through long-term independent work in handmade hair accessories and phone cases, with careful attention to material choice, colour coordination, and detailed finishing. Further photography training with professional photographers introduced a stronger awareness of composition and light, which has since informed the design process. Craft is viewed as a way to bring warmth, personal aesthetics, and care into everyday objects through making.
